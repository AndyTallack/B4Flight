Get Started with B4Flight in 4 Steps

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

STEP 1: Start your briefing

From your dashboard, you can start your briefing in 3 different ways:

  • Upload a flight plan you've created in other software
  • Choose a route you've previously saved
  • Create a new route by entering waypoints

TRY IT:

  1. Click on Create

STEP 2: Enter a route

Enter details of your flight route, so that B4Flight can identify the NOTAMS relevant to your flight. B4Flight recognises waypoints set out in the CAA AIP - aerodromes, navaids, fixes.

TRY IT:

  1. Enter a name and description for your flight, then the waypoints. Each time you press the space-bar, B4Flight validates your route and updates the map.
  2. Click on Save & Brief to save your flight, and view relevant NOTAMS

STEP 3: Review NOTAMS

Review the NOTAMS on the map interactively.

TRY IT:

  1. Click on a NOTAM to see the details. You can hide NOTAMs for this session, or permanently
  2. Filter NOTAMS by category, by radius, or by planned flight date
  3. Click on Print Briefing... to view a printable briefing document.

STEP 4: Print your briefing

Your briefing document separates NOTAMS relevant to departure, en-route, and destination.

TRY IT:

  1. Choose whether to include NOTAMS you've hidden
  2. Use your browser's print functionality to print your briefing
